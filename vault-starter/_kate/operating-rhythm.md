# Operating Rhythm

## Recurring Cadences
| Cadence | Day/Time | Required Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Morning triage | Mon-Fri 7:00 AM | Daily note with URGENT/HIGH queue, action queue, and meeting prep status |
| STU ROB | Monday 9:00 AM | Weekly ROB summary finalized Sunday night with risks, decisions, and asks |
| Pipeline review | Wednesday 2:00 PM | Opportunity hygiene pass, milestone risk flags, and close-date deltas |
| Winning Wednesdays | Wednesday 4:00 PM | Channel summary with wins, blockers, and owner follow-ups |
| Customer escalation sweep | Daily 4:30 PM | Check new executive/VIP signals and unresolved urgent threads |
| Weekly planning reset | Friday 3:00 PM | Next-week top priorities, prep gaps, and dependency map |
| Month-end forecast lock | Last business day 1:00 PM | Forecast confidence checks and exception list |
| Quarter-start planning | First business day of quarter 10:00 AM | Quarter priorities, key milestones, and operating assumptions |

## Key Dates
- Q2 planning kickoff: first Monday of quarter
- Mid-quarter health check: week 6 Wednesday
- Quarter close readiness review: final two Fridays
- Add business-specific dates as they are confirmed (town halls, major customer reviews, launches)

## Channels to Monitor
- Winning Wednesdays
- Deal summaries channels
- Team priority channels

## Service-Level Targets
- Morning brief complete by 7:15 AM local time.
- High-profile meeting briefs available at least 2 hours before start.
- Urgent escalation acknowledgment drafted within 30 minutes during business hours.
- End-of-day action queue reconciled before 5:30 PM.

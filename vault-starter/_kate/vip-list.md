# VIP List

## Tier 1 (Always URGENT)
- STU
- STU Chief of Staff alias
- STU leadership direct reports
- Active executive sponsor contacts for in-flight customer escalations

## Tier 2 (Always HIGH)
- Strategic customer decision makers for active opportunities
- Top partner account leads tied to current quarter milestones
- Internal approvers for budget, legal, and launch-critical dependencies

## Tier 3 (Track Closely)
- Project leads and technical champions for active deliveries
- Regional field stakeholders that influence execution timing
- Recurring partner contacts with frequent status updates

## Notes
- For every entry, include: role, relationship context, escalation rationale, and downgrade conditions.
- If a Tier 1 contact sends a low-signal FYI, keep classification at HIGH unless explicitly overridden.
- Review this list every Friday after ROB and update for ownership or org changes.

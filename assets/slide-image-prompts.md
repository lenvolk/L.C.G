# Personal Chief of Staff Deck Image Brief

This deck already has strong structure and clean typography. The images should add atmosphere and metaphor, not compete with the copy.

## Overall Visual Direction

- Style: premium enterprise editorial illustration with subtle 3D depth, Microsoft Fluent-inspired surfaces, clean geometry, no cartoon look
- Mood: calm control replacing operational chaos
- Palette: deep navy, Microsoft blue, teal, purple, orange, and green accents on soft neutral backgrounds
- Avoid: generic robots, stock-photo business handshakes, obvious sci-fi holograms, cluttered dashboards, anime aesthetics, overly dark cyberpunk scenes
- Composition: prioritize negative space and simple focal points; most slides should use right-side or background imagery so the text remains primary
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 widescreen
- Rendering note: ask for polished lighting, soft shadows, crisp edges, and presentation-safe readability

## Recommended Workflow

1. Generate the Priority 1 pack first.
2. Choose one consistent rendering style before doing Slide 4 to Slide 6.
3. Keep dense slides subtle; those images should sit behind content, not compete with it.
4. Once the files exist, wire them into the deck with conservative sizing and opacity.
